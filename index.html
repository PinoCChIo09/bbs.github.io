Our Vision

Founded by three passionate game developers who met during their university days, Bond Breaker Studios was born from a shared love for aquatic environments and role-playing adventures.

With Raah: A Fisherman's Journey, we're combining stunning visuals with deep RPG mechanics to create a mobile experience that transports players to serene yet adventurous waters.
