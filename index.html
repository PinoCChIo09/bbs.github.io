Game Features

Discover what makes RAAH a unique fishing adventure

Journey Through Forgotten Rivers

Traverse ancient waters that hold echoes of Raghvan’s past. Each river tells a story — of loss, memory, and the mysteries that flow beneath its surface.

The Search for His Son

Guided by visions and fragments of hope, Raghvan follows the currents in search of his missing son. Every choice brings him closer to the truth — or deeper into the unknown.

The Spirits of the Deep

The waters are alive with whispers of gods and ghosts. Earn the favor of elemental spirits, uncover their secrets, and face what lies beyond the veil of the living.

Craft Your Legacy

Embark on Raghvan's journey through sacred rivers and forgotten lands, guided by ancient runes that shape his fate. Raah is still in development, with its world and story evolving with each tide.

  • Story-driven exploration inspired by Indian mythology
  • Runes that changes combat and abilities
  • A touching journey of love, family, and the search for purpose.

Our Vision

Bond Breaker Studios started on a simple coffee break. Three friends, a shared love for gaming, and a spark of “let's make something of our own.” We had limited know-how, but our passion was limitless.

With Raah: A Fisherman's Journey, we're bringing players into serene yet adventurous waters, blending stunning visuals with deep RPG gameplay. It's our love for games, turned into an experience we hope you'll never forget.
